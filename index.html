S1 — NegRisk Arbitrage
Mathematical arb · $30 allocation · Win rate ~100%
Active
Scans NegRisk groups where sum(YES ask prices) < 1.0. Buying all YES outcomes guarantees exactly one pays out $1.00 — the profit is the gap between cost and payout. Phantom arb filter: legs must be priced 0.03–0.97 (not already resolved), total_ask must be 0.50–0.99, and each leg must have ≥$1,000 volume/day.
