P&L Math — Canonical Formulas
shares = cost ÷ buy_price
fee = 2.25×(p×(1−p))²×cost
edge = win_prob − buy_price
WIN pnl = shares×$1 − cost − fee
LOSS pnl = −(cost + fee)
ROI = pnl ÷ cost × 100
EV = p×(1/price−1) − (1−p) − fee
Kelly = ((p×b−q)/b) × 0.25
b = (1 − buy_price) ÷ buy_price
