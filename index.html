S1 — NegRisk Arbitrage
Mathematical arb · Win rate ~100%
Active
Scans NegRisk groups where the sum of all YES ask prices is below 1.0. Buying all YES outcomes guarantees exactly one pays out — the implied profit is the gap between what you paid and $1.00. Filters: legs must be 0.03–0.97 (not already resolved), total_ask must be 0.50–0.99, volume ≥ $1,000/day.

S10 — Near-Resolution Harvest
Probabilistic · Win rate 88–95% target
Active
Finds markets where one outcome is 85–98.9% certain and resolves within 7 days. The edge comes from the market still trading below the true probability — usually due to liquidity thin spots or slow price discovery. Filters: volume ≥ $1,000/day, spread ≤ 5%, price strictly below 0.989 (excludes already-resolved markets).

S11 — In-Play Momentum
NEW · Momentum · Building data
Active
Detects sports markets moving >20% in the same direction across multiple Observer snapshots. Buys into the momentum when price is 0.60–0.92 (still has room to run to 0.99 at final whistle). Discovered from live data: Real Madrid moved +55% in 56 minutes during a live game. Needs 2+ Observer cycles to build price history before firing.
