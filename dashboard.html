How the math works on every trade
Entry: shares = cost ÷ buy_price  |  fee = 2.25 × (p×(1-p))² × cost  |  edge = win_probability − buy_price
Win: pnl = shares × $1.00 − cost − fee  |  Loss: pnl = −(cost + fee)
ROI = pnl ÷ cost × 100%  |  EV = p×(1/price−1) − (1−p) − fee_rate  |  Kelly = ((p×b−q)/b) × 0.25 where b = 1/price−1
